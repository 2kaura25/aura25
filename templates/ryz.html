RYZ-N-FALL



A test of agility and coordination where players must balance and strategize to stay on top.

Rules and Regulations

1. Each team must have 3 members.

2.The participants should register their names via online.

3.A classic board game with spin wheel instead of dice.

4.Spin the wheel: odd number = technical question, even number = non-technical question..

5. Wrong or unanswered questions move the team two points backward.

6. Hitting a shockwave requires completing a dare; failure means starting over from the shockwave.

7.First team to reach 100 wins. - Time Limit: 20 minutes.
